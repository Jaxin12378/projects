Action film

In a world where shadows move faster than light and screams echo longer than gunshots, survival is not just a fight — it’s a war. When a covert military experiment goes horribly wrong, a remote town is plunged into chaos, overrun by creatures born of science and nightmares. Trapped and hunted, a group of strangers must fight their way through the darkness — armed with nothing but instinct, courage, and dwindling ammo. As the line between man and monster blurs , one truth becomes clear: the real horror isn’t what’s out there… it’s what’s inside.
